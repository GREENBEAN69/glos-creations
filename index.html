Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Glo.

Glo's Creations 3:16 began at a small kitchen table with a block of clay and an idea — that jewelry should feel as personal as the person wearing it.

Every piece in this shop is hand-sculpted in small batches. No two are exactly alike. I love working with neutral, soft palettes that go with everything and feel quietly special.

Thank you for supporting handmade.

Virtual Try-On

How it works:

1. Upload a front-facing photo or use your camera
2. Our AI will detect your face and place the earrings
3. Drag the earrings to adjust their position
4. Use the slider to resize and zoom to see detail
5. Save the image to share or keep

💡 Tips for best results:

Face the camera directly · Use good lighting · Pull hair behind ears

📸

Upload a photo of yourself

3 Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
